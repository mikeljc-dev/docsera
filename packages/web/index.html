How it works

Classic RAG, engineered to be boring and reliable.

Ingest

Docsera crawls your sitemap or takes your Markdown, splits it into sections, and stores embeddings in Postgres with pgvector — one service for data and vectors.

Retrieve

Each question is embedded and matched against your docs by cosine similarity. If nothing relevant passes the threshold, it answers "I don't know" without even calling the LLM.

Answer with citations

The LLM answers strictly from the retrieved context, and the widget shows the sources — deep links to the exact sections, like /docs/install#requirements.
